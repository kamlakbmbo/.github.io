Kamlak bmbo
 

Kamlak Bmbo is an instrumental artist from Ethiopia, whose real name is Kamlak Taye. He is known for his unique sound that blends Ethiopian traditional music with modern and contemporary elements. Kamlak began his musical journey in 2019 and has since been making a name for himself in the Ethiopian music scene as a rising star in the instrumental music genre.
